What is DrinkEntrepreneurs?

DrinkEntrepreneurs is a monthly event where entrepreneurs connect in a casual environment. We don't just network - we make friends in the startup world. We run in Shenzhen as well as Paris, London, San Francisco, Shanghai and many other cities. Thousands of entrepreneurs have met partners, co-workers, investors and friends through the event.

DrinkEntrepreneurs in San Francisco

Watch the video on YouTube if not working on Youku.

DrinkEntrepreneurs is different from other business events because it's laid back and informal. It's a Silicon Valley cultural export. It's like having a beer with friends, not like overtime. It's non-profit - we do it for fun and love of the game. There are lots of startupers young and old, and you don't wear a tie unless you plan to use it for a lap dance!

Frequently Asked Questions

Who pays for the drinks?

The event is free but drinks are not included. Since the venue is really nice and we don't want to be squatters, we require our attendees to buy at least 1 drink. The good thing is, our attendees get promotional prices on food & drinks!

Do I have to register?

Yes, you have to register because the number of attendees is limited. Register for free here (BIG Blue Button!).

Can I come if I'm not an entrepreneur?

You don't have to be an entrepreneur as in "I own a business". We welcome any entrepreneur-minded people: wannabe-entrepreneurs, students in entrepreneurship, people often working with entrepreneurs, involved in many associative projects, or English teachers with big ambitions... In a nutshell, anybody trying to build things and shake up the world.

What language will we speak?

The event is open to speakers of English & Chinese/Cantonese alike. There will be Chinese and international entrepreneurs. Our hosts are multi-lingual, and lots of people will be fluent in both languages. DrinkEntrepreneurs is hosted in many countries, we deeply want to connect bright people from different cultures.
